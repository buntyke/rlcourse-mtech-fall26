**Welcome & Course Orientation**

This session introduces the Reinforcement Learning paradigm — the agent, environment, reward, and the learning loop — and situates it within the broader landscape of machine learning. We also walk through the course structure, objectives, assessment plan, and key resources.

**Topics:**
- What is Reinforcement Learning? How is it different from supervised and unsupervised learning?
- Key concepts: agent, environment, state, action, reward, policy
- Famous RL milestones: TD-Gammon, AlphaGo, OpenAI Five, ChatGPT's RLHF
- Course roadmap: Units I–V, tutorials, assessments
- Textbook and online resources
- Lab setup: Python, Gymnasium

**Reference:** Sutton & Barto, *RL: An Introduction*, Ch. 1
